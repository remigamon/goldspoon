ABOUT US

GoldSpoon Restaurant offers premium food at reasonable prices. Owned by a Malay Muslim entrepreneur, At first glance, you might think we're an expensive fine-dining spot or even a Chinese restaurant because of our cozy atmosphere and top-notch facilities. But we are all about great food, comfort, and affordability. Whether you're here for a casual meal or a special occasion, we promise a dining experience that feels luxurious without the high price.
